PART 2: PVP SERVERS While PVP servers such as 2b2tpvp, crystalpvp, and the voco pvp server are good for practice, they are not representative of actual 2b2t (and to a lesser scale, constantiam) combat. These servers assume perfect-world conditions, with little to no lag and glitches. I recommend that you try pvping on 2b2t (at 0,0, for example) to get a feel for what the server is really like. Often times, consumables like chorus and gapples don't work, lag spikes happen that screw you up, etc. So, my advice is to divide time between pvping on practice servers and the real deal equally. This way, you will have a better feel for real combat and will be better at PVP for when it's required.(edited)
