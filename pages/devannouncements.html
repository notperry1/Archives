coltsnid 15-Feb-20 05:57 PM
If anyone experiences any of the following problems, please report them in #bugs, as these are the major issues that are holding back VoQueue from moving into Beta/being released: - "Insta-DC" bug: You are kicked back to the VoQueue hub with the message "Connection reset by 2b2t! Transferring to VoQueue Hub...". While the message says this is 2b2t, it is actually VoQueue causing the disconnect. Your account stays in-game, you just can't log in. - "Account Lockout" bug: You start receiving the Error notification "VoQueue was unable to login into your Minecraft account" every time AutoQueue tries to queue your account. Something is causing Mojang to reset VoQueue account passwords. These accounts must have their passwords reset through a recovery email. - General AFK instability: This could be a combination of several bugs, like some of which we have found over the past few weeks. Basically random small bugs that cause VoQueue accounts to get stuck, crash, and/or disconnect. For instance, we found an issue recently where making contact with certain blocks like fences (while AFK) would cause accounts to disconnect, because VoQueue was using incorrect block physics. There may be dozens of random small bugs like this (which we don't know about) that can cause accounts to disconnect, since by definition it will be from circumstances that are not common, but do occur.
