Welcome to the Base Director team! The role of a VoCo Base Director is setting up, assigning members to, and managing bases on 2b2t or Constantiam (mainly 2b2t). As a Base Director, you are expected to consistently run a minimum of 2 active bases at any given time, except when first starting. To set up a VoCo Base, just follow these simple guidelines: 1. Check the VoCo Base Director Application Spreadsheet, you will need to be given perms to edit by a @Base Supervisor, this will require a Gmail account. 2. Find 3-5 VoCo Members who want to start a base using the VoCo Base Application Spreadsheet, try to group members together based on trust and experience in VoCo (less trusted/less experienced people should be assigned to less important bases). 3. Once you've chosen your members, tag a @Base Supervisor in #director_chat to open a base channel. They'll give you perms to add the members you've chosen to the channel. Mark members you claim according to the Spreadsheet legend and leave a comment including which base they were assigned to. 4. Pick a set of Coordinates that is secure. Make sure your coordinates aren't too close to spawn or any axis/highway (it'll be found easily), or too far (your members won't be able to get to the base). Use the guide below to gauge how far from spawn your base should be depending on the base level. 5. Ensure that the planned base location is not nearby any other portals or bases in the nether. Build the base at least 600 blocks from the portal in the overworld, if you have a portal at the base itself it must be a one way portal (no going from the nether directly into the base). 6. Give your members the coordinates to start building. You are allowed to pin the coords to the base channel, but you may DM them privately to individual members for extra security if you wish.
(edited)
Base Distance Guide: Nether Coords; each axis should roughly fall into the listed range. Initiate-Level Bases: 3k-10k Member-Level Bases: 5k-20k Member+-Level Bases: 10k-25k VIP-Level Bases: 15k-30k Eminent-Level Bases: 20k-35k Elite-Level Bases: 25k-40k Exalted+-Level Bases: 30k-50k+ This is simply a guide meant to give you an idea on approximate base distance depending on Base Level and anticipated risk. As Base Director, base security is entirely up to you. If you follow these guidelines and use common sense, your base should be at less risk of being leaked or griefed.
